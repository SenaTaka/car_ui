See — and hear — your car in real time.

Connect an ELM327 Bluetooth (BLE) adapter and car_ui turns live OBD2 data into a fast, modern dashboard: RPM, speed, coolant, throttle, fuel, and every sensor your vehicle supports, auto-detected. No subscription. Ever.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT
• A genuinely modern interface — not the dated look of most OBD apps
• A one-of-a-kind engine-sound simulation driven by your real RPM
• Speed and RPM drawn on the map, color-coded (satellite view included)
• Buy once for Pro — no monthly fees, unlike many scanners

BUILD YOUR OWN DASHBOARD
Mix and match four widget types — digital tiles, analog needle gauges, live charts, and the trip map — then reorder them into your own instrument screen. A mirrored HUD mode projects your speed onto the windshield at night.

FIVE FOCUSED TABS
• Dashboard — your own widget layout, plus HUD mode
• Data — every live sensor, overlaid or separate charts, CSV export (long or spreadsheet-friendly wide format)
• Drive — trip computer with fuel economy, the color-coded track map, a G-force ball, and 0–100 timing
• Engine Sound — procedural sound that follows your real RPM (all presets free)
• Tools — read and clear trouble codes (DTC), raw commands, communication log

NO ACCOUNT, NO TRACKING
car_ui talks only to your ELM327 adapter over Bluetooth. There is no developer server for your car data. Try it with no hardware using the built-in demo mode.

REQUIRES an ELM327-compatible Bluetooth LE (BLE) OBD2 adapter and a vehicle with an OBD2 port (most cars sold since 2008).
